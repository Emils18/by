Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. Keep chat language clean and appropriate for all ages. No swearing, offensive language, or spamming.
  2. No griefing or stealing from other players' builds. Respect each other's creations and property.
  3. PvP (Player vs Player) is allowed only if both parties consent. No random or excessive PvP without permission.
  4. Build within designated areas or follow server guidelines for claiming land. No building or mining in restricted zones.
  5. No cheating, hacking, or exploiting glitches. Play fair and enjoy the game as intended.
  6. Do not intentionally lag or crash the server. This includes excessive redstone contraptions or entity farms.
  7. Use common sense and be a positive member of the community. Help others, collaborate on projects, and foster a friendly environment for all players.
  8. Follow any additional rules or instructions provided by server staff. Their decisions are final in all matters related to gameplay and server management.

Discord server

  1. Be respectful to everyone.
  2. No spamming or excessive messaging.
  3. Keep discussions appropriate for all ages.
  4. Use the correct channels for specific topics.
  5. No sharing personal information.
  6. Follow the server’s guidelines and decisions made by moderators.
  7. No harassment or hate speech.

Feel free to adjust these to fit your server’s community!

More rules

  1. No NSFW Content: Keep content family-friendly and appropriate for all members.
  2. Report Issues: If you see rule-breaking, report it to a moderator.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
